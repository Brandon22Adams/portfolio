What is Hytale World gen v2?

World gen v2 is their second iteration on how worlds are built within Hytale. With this system of tools people can make extremely diverse and beautiful landscapes that are randomly generated.

How I built Hytale Mods.

Hytale has created a system to generate biomes using a series of nodes! Using simplex, and cell noise lots of math nodes some really incredible terrain shapes can be created. There is an extensive material system, prefabs, and environmental variables on top to make truly unique worlds. Making worlds has been a blast!

What I learned.

The biggest hurdle I have had to overcome and learn was how to manipulate noise into different shapes. All of their tooling and naming conventions were pretty straight forward but noise has been a tricky one to learn. Its been the perfect balance of technical problem solving and visual feedback. Its why I fell in love with Frontend Development and its why I have been enjoying building biomes.
